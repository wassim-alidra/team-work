Department of Agricultural Resource Management

October 14, 2023 at 10:42 AM

To All Registered Operators in Region B,

Following the recent environmental impact assessments conducted across key watersheds in Region B, the Ministry is implementing immediate adjustments to synthetic fertilizer distribution quotas for the third quarter.

Key Adjustments:

  • Nitrogen-based allowances reduced by 12% against baseline allocation.
  • Phosphorus-based allowances capped strictly at 45kg per hectare.
  • Organic compost supplements are now subsidized at an increased rate of 30% to offset synthetic reductions.

These measures are critical to maintaining soil health stability and ensuring long-term yield viability. Your digital inventory systems will automatically reflect these new caps within 48 hours.

Failure to adhere to the revised quotas upon next marketplace purchase will result in an automated hold on your procurement license pending ministerial review.

For detailed methodology regarding the assessment, or to file for an exception based on specific topographical needs, please access the full report below.
